Gnocchi with burnt butter and walnuts

20m prep | 15m cook | 4 servings

A delicious restaurant-style dish made simple enough, you can easily make it at home

Description:

Health Score: 4.7

Calories per serve: 336

Allergens: Recipe may contain tree nut, milk and lactose.

This Gnocchi with burnt butter and walnuts is a decadent comfort food dish that takes less than 15 minutes to make.

The holiday season is a busy time for everyone, so a dish like this Gnocchi with walnuts is just waht you need on the menu. It's a savory dish that comes together in less than 15 minutes, only requires a handful of ingredients and the nutty, sweet tatse of the brown butter to truly delight your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 quantity Basics Potato Gnocchi
  • 125g butter, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sage leaves
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 120g pkt Coles Australian Baby Spinach
  • 1/2 cup (50g) walnuts, toasted, chopped
  • Grated parmesan, to serve

Steps:

    Step 1

  1. Line a large baking tray with baking paper. Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil. Add one-quarter of gnocchi. Cook for 2-3 mins or until the gnocchi rise to surfaces of the pan. Use a slotted spoon transfer to lined tray. Repeat, in 3 batches, with remaining gnocchi.

    2. Step 2

  2. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a large frying pan over medium heat. Cook for 2-3 mins or until the butter melts and begins to foam. Add the sage and cook for 1-2 mins or until crisp. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the sage to a plate.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add the garlic, gnocchi and spinach to the pan. Season. Cook, tossing, until the gnocchi is coated in butter mixture and the spinach wilts.

    4. Step 4

  4. Divide the gnocchi mixture among serving bowls. Sprinkle with fried sage and walnuts. Sprinkle with parmesan.
